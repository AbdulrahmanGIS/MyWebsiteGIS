About Us

I have strong experience in managing GIS databases, analyzing them, and presenting them in various ways. Additionally, I am skilled in converting data formats between different software platforms.
Above all, I have a deep passion for GIS, I truly love this career.

  • I graduated from Alexandria University, majoring in GIS.
  • Trainee at Esri North Africa, Penta-b, and CAD Master.
  • GIS Analyst at CRV Infrastructure Projects.

As for my experience, I have worked on various projects and with different organizations since graduating.

  • My current role at CRV involves working on projects for replacing and renewing electricity networks in Kafr El Dawwar and Abu Al Matamir in Beheira Governorate, as well as contributing to the electricity infrastructure project for New Al Fardous City, Cairo.
  • Previously, I worked as a GIS Data Specialist for the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, where I managed geospatial data for the State Assets Governance Project (NSDI).
  • At the Alexandria Governorate Office, I contributed to the strategic plan for Alexandria 2032 by preparing and digitizing centralized geodatabases, ensuring data accuracy and reliability.
  • Additionally, during my freelance work with the NEOM NEN PRC 002 project, I specialized in data intake, validation, cleaning, transformation (ETL), geodatabase design, and quality assurance.

These roles have strengthened my expertise in GIS analysis, geodatabase management, and the implementation of innovative geospatial solutions.

Services

Check our Services.

Data Intake and Validation.

Collecting and verifying data to ensure it meets required standards, identifying errors or inconsistencies, and preparing it for further processing.

Data Transformation.

Converting data between different formats, coordinate systems, or structures to ensure compatibility with GIS platforms and project requirements.

Geodatabase Design and Creation.

Developing geodatabases tailored to project needs, optimizing the storage, organization, and retrieval of spatial and attribute data.

Data Import, Organization, and Editing.

Importing raw datasets, structuring them systematically, and performing necessary edits to enhance usability and accuracy.

Metadata Creation and Attribute Addition.

Creating detailed metadata for datasets to ensure proper documentation and adding attributes to enrich data with relevant contextual information.

Quality Assurance and Quality Control (QA/QC).

Implementing robust QA/QC processes to validate data accuracy, completeness, and consistency, ensuring high-quality outputs for analysis and decision-making.
